FAQ

  • -Whats your delivery area?

    We deliver at Downtown area of Tel Aviv-Yafo. From north Yehuda Hamaccabi to Tsahalon on south and Ayalon Hwy on east.

  • -What are you prices?

    Our price range can vary from 30 for Falafel bowl and up to 130 for Hotdish.

  • -Whats your minimum order?

    We don't have such thing as "minimum order". You can order anything from our menu and we still gonna deliver it to you despite of amount!

  • -Can I schedule my order?

    Yes, you can! Just contact us via phone or contact form to place your order remotely or go through ordering process and you'll have an option to change delivery time.

  • -How much time it takes to make and deliver my order?

    Depends on size of your order. It can vary from 30 minutes to few hours because we want you to have your order as fresh as possible.

  • -I didnt find my question, who can I contact?

    You can use contact form at the bottom of page, email us, give us a call or request a callback and we will contact you within 20 minutes during working hours.
